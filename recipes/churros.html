Churros Recipe

Description

Crunchy and sweet churros ready to be your day and nighttime snacks

Referenced from: Allrecipes Churros II Recipe

Ingredient List

  • 2 quarts vegetable oil
  • 1 cup of water
  • 1/2 cup margarine
  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 3 eggs
  • 1/4 cup white sugar
  • 1/4 teaspoon ground cinnamon

Steps

  1. Heat oil (should be about 1 1/2 inches deep) to 360 degree F (180 degrees C) in a heavy deep skillet or deep-fryer.
  2. In a medium saucepan, heat water and margarine to a rolling boil
  3. Combine flour and salt and stir into boiling mixture
  4. Reduce heat to low and stir vigorously until mixture forms a ball about 1 minute
  5. Remove from heat and beaet in the eggs one at a time
  6. Spoon mixture into pastry bag fitted with a large star tip
  7. Squeeze out 4 inch long strips of dough directly into hot oil
  8. Fry 3 or 4 strips at once until golden brown (about 2 minutes each side)
  9. Remove from hot oil to drain on paper towels
  10. Stir together the sugar and cinnamon
  11. Roll churros in the mixture while hot and enjoy!
