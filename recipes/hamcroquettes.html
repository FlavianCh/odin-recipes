Ham Croquettes Recipe

Description

Delicious and mouthwatering Ham Croquttes, make you crave even more

Referenced from: Allrecipes Ham Croquettes Recipe

Ingredient List

  • 1 tablespoon butter
  • 1/2 cup onion
  • 3 medium green onions
  • 1/2 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1 (16 ounce) package sliced honey ham
  • 1 1/2 cups shredded Cheddar cheese
  • 3 cups dried breadcrumbs
  • Salt and pepper
  • 4 large eggs
  • 2 cups water oil
  • 1/8 cup of water

Steps

  1. Prepare the Ingredients:
    • chop the onion and the green onions
    • finely chop the honey ham
    • divide breadcrumbs
  2. Melt butter in large skillet over medium heat
  3. Stir in onion, green onions, and garlic
  4. Cook until tender (about 10 minutes)
  5. Transfer onion mixture into a large bowl
  6. Stir in ham, cheddar cheese, and 1 1/2 cups of bread crumbs
  7. Beat 3 eggs, stir into ham mixture
  8. Sprinkle with salt and paper
  9. Using hand, make golf ball-size balls out of the ham mixture
  10. Heat vegetable oil in a large skillet over medium heat
  11. Spread remaining bread crumbs onto a plate
  12. In a large bowl, beat 1 egg, stir in water
  13. Dip balls into egg mixture and roll into bread crumbs
  14. Place ball in hot oil
  15. Fry until golden brown
  16. Remove to paper towels to soak up grease and enjoy!
